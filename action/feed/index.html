Gameplay

  • Difficult but rewarding: Levels are challenging, but completing them is very satisfying.
  • Two game modes:
    • Normal Mode: Reach the end without crashing to complete the level.

Your Character

  • You can unlock new icons and colors to customize your character.
  • Throughout a level, your character transforms into different shapes with unique movements:
    • Cube: Jumps when you tap/click.
    • Ship: Flies up when you hold, down when you release.
    • Ball: Rolls and flips gravity with each tap/click.
    • UFO: Makes small jumps when tapped, falls otherwise.
    • Wave: Flies in a zigzag pattern, tap/click to go up, release to go down.
    • Robot: Jumps higher the longer you hold.
    • Spider: Teleports to the nearest ceiling and flips gravity when tapped.

  • Practice makes perfect: Don’t get discouraged by difficulty. Practice levels to improve your timing and memorize obstacles.
  • Use Practice Mode: Set checkpoints to focus on specific parts of a level.
  • Listen to the music: The music often cues upcoming obstacles.
  • Focus: Reduce or mute the music for better concentration if needed.
  • Unlock customization options: Keep playing to unlock new character looks.

Additional Features

  • 21 Levels: Each with unique music and challenges (Full version offers more).

Ready to jump in?
