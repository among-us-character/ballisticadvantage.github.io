Dive into World Creation with Little Alchemy and Little Alchemy 2!

Little Alchemy and Little Alchemy 2 are captivating games that challenge you to build a universe, element by element. By combining basic starting elements, you’ll unlock hundreds of increasingly complex items, from familiar objects like water and fire to fantastical creatures like dragons and unicorns.

Getting Started:

Both games share the core concept of element combination. Here’s a quick rundown:

  1. Starting Elements: You begin with fundamental elements like air, water, earth, and fire.
  2. Drag and Drop: Drag one element onto another to attempt a combination. If successful, a new element appears.
  3. Experimentation is Key! There’s no set path. Try combining different elements to discover new creations.

Little Alchemy vs Little Alchemy 2:

While the core gameplay remains the same, there are a few key differences:

  • Accessibility: Little Alchemy is free to play online, while Little Alchemy 2 is a paid mobile app.
  • Interface: Little Alchemy has a simpler interface for dragging and dropping elements. Little Alchemy 2 offers a toolbar with search functions, saving options, and more.
  • Content: Little Alchemy 2 boasts more elements, dynamic visuals, and an expanded library to explore.

Tips and Tricks:

  • Think logically: Combine elements that seem thematically linked. For example, water and earth might create mud.
  • Pay attention to the encyclopedia: Both games offer descriptions of the elements you create. These descriptions can provide clues for further combinations.
  • Don’t be afraid to revisit old elements: Combining previously created elements can unlock new discoveries.
  • Utilize online resources: For those stuck, there are online guides (like [Little Alchemy Cheats]) that offer complete walkthroughs and element combination recipes.

Beyond the Basics: Little Alchemy 2 Specifics

Little Alchemy 2 offers additional features to enhance your world-building experience:

  • Collections: Organize your creations into themed collections for easier navigation.
  • Search function: Quickly find specific elements within your growing library.
  • Animations: Witness some elements come to life with charming animations.

Ready to Begin?

So, are you ready to craft your own universe? With a little creativity and these helpful tips, you’ll be well on your way to becoming a master alchemist in both Little Alchemy and Little Alchemy 2. Remember, experimentation is key, so have fun and unleash your inner creator!

little alchemy hints

  • Think logically: Combine elements that seem thematically related. Fire + Water = Steam, Earth + Water = Mud, etc.
  • Experiment with existing elements: Once you have a decent number of elements, try combining them in various ways. You might be surprised by the results!
  • Unlock hidden elements: Discovering some elements unlocks new base elements like “Light” or “Time.” These can open up new creation possibilities.

how to make star in little alchemy 2

Little Alchemy 2 requires you to unlock “Time” before creating a Star. You’ll need to discover at least 100 elements first. Here’s a streamlined recipe:

  1. Combine Air + Air = Pressure
  2. Combine Earth + Pressure = Stone
  3. Combine Earth + Earth = Land
  4. Combine Land + Land = Continent
  5. Combine Continent + Continent = Planet
  6. Combine Stone + Planet = Moon (Alternatively, Earth + Water = Ocean, Ocean + Sand = Beach, Beach + Stone = Moon)
  7. Unlock “Time” (requires 100+ elements discovered)
  8. Combine Time + Moon = Night
  9. Combine Night + Sky (Sky: Sun + Atmosphere – see below) = Star


how to make night in little alchemy

  1. You can obtain Night using the Star creation method above (steps 7 & 8).
  2. Alternatively, combine Sun + Cloud = Rain. Rain interacts with various elements to create Night. Try Rain with:
    • Land
    • Forest (Tree + Land)
    • Mountain (Earth + Fire)
