]]> The popular football simulator 4th and Goal is now available as a free version built for web browsers and mobile devices. Developed by Glowmonkey Studios, 4th and Goal lets you experience the thrill of football gameplay and the drama of driving down the field against a tough defense to score the winning touchdown.

Simple Controls and Addictive Gameplay

4th and Goal features simple swipe or tap-based controls that make it easy to pass, run, kick and more. Gameplay moves at a fast pace using an overhead view of the field. AI opponents provide a solid challenge as you navigate down the field. Successfully completing first downs and touchdowns is highly satisfying. An arcade-like feel and short game times make it easy to pick up and play.

Play as Both Offense and Defense

You can choose to play as either the offense or defense and both are equally fun. Playing as the quarterback and receivers on offense, pull off precision passes and jukes to make big plays. As the defense, try to outmaneuver opponents to sack the QB, intercept passes or prevent touchdowns. Football fan or not, the gameplay is accessible to all.

Options for Everyone

Whether you prefer single player, 2 player local PVP or online multiplayer, 4th and Goal has options for everyone. Take on AI opponents of varying difficulties, go head to head with friends or play anonymously online against people all over the world. 4 player PVP promotes chaotic on-field action and comebacks.

there are also lots of teams to choose from including official pro teams from the NFL.

Overall, 4th and Goal Football succeeds at delivering an enjoyable football experience on the go or in your browser. Its simple design and intuitive interface combined with competitive gameplay options for all will have you chanting ‘Go for it on 4th and Goal!’. Best of all, you can now experience everything at no cost. For some free football fun, check out 4th and Goal today!

What are the system requirements for playing 4th and Goal?

The system requirements for playing 4th and Goal are very minimal since it is a casual browser/mobile game:

For Mobile (Android and iOS):

  • Requires iOS 9.0 or later. Compatible with iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch.
  • Requires Android 4.1 and up. Compatible with most Android phones and tablets.
  • Mobile data or WiFi connection required to play online multiplayer. Offline single player modes also available.

For Web Browsers (PC/Mac):

  • Requires a PC or Mac running Windows 7+, macOS 10.9+ or Chrome OS.
  • Requires an internet connection to load the game in your browser. Offline play not available.
  • Works in most modern web browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, etc.
  • Does not require any downloads, plugins or installs. The game is played directly in your browser using HTML5.
  • Integrated graphics including Intel HD Graphics are sufficient. A dedicated GPU is not needed.
  • 2 GHz or faster processor. 4th and Goal gameplay is not CPU intensive.
  • 4GB RAM minimum for browsers to run smoothly. More memory is better.
  • Not compatible with Windows XP or Vista. Windows 7 or newer recommended.
  • Free browser storage required. The game itself does not take up any hard drive space.

Overall, the requirements to play 4th and Goal are quite basic. As long as you have a modern device with an updated OS and browsers, you should have no problem loading and playing 4th and Goal football on the go or on your PC/Mac. The fact that no downloads are needed also makes it very convenient to just pick up and play anywhere. Let the games begin!

Can I play multiplayer with friends?

Yes, 4th and Goal offers several multiplayer options to play with friends:

  • Local PVP – Play against a friend on the same device in 2 player split-screen. Pass and play the device between drives.
  • Online PVP – Invite a friend or match with another player anonymously online. Play in real-time to beat your opponent. A WiFi connection is required.
  • Broadcast PVP – One player streams their match live for up to 3 spectators. Spectators can cheer on players and chat during the stream. The broadcaster must have a strong internet connection to stream.

With fun and social multiplayer, 4th and Goal is perfect for some casual football competition with friends on the go or at home. Give these options a try!

Is 4th and Goal free to play?

Yes, 4th and Goal is now available as a completely free game on iOS, Android and web browsers. All game modes and content are available at no cost.

Can I play 4th and Goal offline?

4th and Goal offers single player modes that can be played offline including Season mode and Practice mode. Multiplayer modes like online PVP require an internet connection.

What football teams are featured in 4th and Goal?

4th and Goal includes official pro teams from the NFL including the New England Patriots, Dallas Cowboys, San Francisco 49ers, Pittsburgh Steelers and more. College teams are not available. You can play as any NFL team in the game.

How do the controls work in 4th and Goal?

4th and Goal uses simple swipe and tap controls that are intuitive to pick up. To pass, swipe on a receiver. To run, tap and hold in an open direction. Tap to kick field goals and punts. Swipe down to select defensive plays. The controls automatically adjust based on whether you are playing offense or defense.
