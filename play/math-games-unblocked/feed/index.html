]]> Granny: A Creepy Escape Room in Video Game Form

Granny is a jump-scare fueled horror game that puts you in a desperate fight for survival.

You wake up trapped in a creepy old house with a not-so-friendly granny hot on your heels. With excellent sound design and a tense atmosphere, Granny will have you on the edge of your seat, frantically searching for clues and hiding from your pursuer.

The core gameplay is suspenseful sneaking and puzzle solving.

You’ll need to scour the house for items and tools to unlock doors and progress. But be warned, Granny has super hearing and will come running at any sound of a dropped object or a creaky floorboard. This constant threat keeps the tension high and makes every move feel risky.

The game offers multiple difficulty settings, with a practice mode that lets you explore the house without Granny chasing you. This is a great way to familiarize yourself with the layout and plan your escape strategy.

How to Play Granny

  • Stay Quiet: This is rule number one! Move slowly, avoid knocking things over, and crouch to muffle your footsteps.
  • Listen Up: Granny’s footsteps are your early warning system. Use them to track her movements and plan your hiding spots.
  • Think Like a MacGyver: You’ll often need to improvise tools by combining everyday objects.
  • Explore Every Nook and Cranny: Keys, tools, and other helpful items are hidden throughout the house.
  • Use Your Inventory Wisely: Some items have multiple uses, so plan ahead before using them.
  • The Clock is Ticking: You only have five days (in-game time) to escape. Don’t waste time wandering aimlessly.

Weapons and crucial objects inside Granny’s house

You can use these items only once per game and for specific purposes:

  • The Winch Handle = draw the bucket from inside the well in the Backyard.
  • The Padlock Code and The Battery = open a special lock on the Main Door.
  • The Remote Control = open the closet under the stairs in the Main Hallway.
  • The Cogwheels = open the Cogwheel Safe inside the Playhouse in the Backyard.
  • The Wrench = install the Car Engine part.
  • The Car Engine = escape via the car.
  • The Spark Plug = escape by car.
  • The Car Battery = place inside the car for one possible escape route.
  • The Meat = distract Granny’s playful Spider.
  • The Bird Seed = distract the crow in the Crow Room to obtain the item from the cage.
  • The Melon = provides several items.

A Few Extra Tips:

  • The teddy bear you find throughout the house can act as a decoy to distract Granny.
  • The lockpicks you find are fragile. Use them only when absolutely necessary.
  • If you get caught, there might be ways to escape Granny’s grasp…but be quick!

Granny is a thrilling horror game that will test your nerves and puzzle-solving skills. With a good dose of patience, resourcefulness, and maybe a little bit of luck, you just might make it out alive!

Please note: This game can be quite scary, so it may not be suitable for younger players or those who don’t enjoy horror games.
