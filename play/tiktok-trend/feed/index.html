how to play Wood Block Tap Away:

The objective: The goal of Wood Block Tap Away is to clear all the blocks from the screen by rotating and pushing them around until they are removed.

Playing blocks: Each block has arrows on it that indicate the direction it can be rotated. To rotate a block, simply tap on it and then tap on the direction you want to rotate it in.

Placing blocks: To move a block, tap and hold it until it starts to glow, and then drag it to the desired location.

Removing blocks: Blocks will be removed from the screen if they are completely surrounded by empty spaces above, below, and on one side.

Special blocks: There are a few special blocks that can be used to your advantage:

Arrow blocks: These blocks have arrows on all sides that can change the direction of neighboring blocks.

Teleport blocks: These blocks can teleport nearby blocks to a new location.

Tips for success:

Plan your moves carefully. Think about where you want to move each block before you start rotating it.
