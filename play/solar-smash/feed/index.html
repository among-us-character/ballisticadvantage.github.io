What makes Draw Climber 2 so great?

  • Simple and addictive gameplay: All you have to do is draw lines on the screen to guide your cube to the finish line. How to play Draw Climber 2

  1. Draw lines to guide your cube. The objective of the game is to draw lines on the screen to guide your cube to the finish line. The cube will follow the lines you draw, so be careful to make sure they are accurate.
  2. Use different shapes to overcome obstacles. In some levels, you will need to use different shapes to overcome obstacles. For example, you may need to draw a ramp to get your cube over a wall, or a bridge to cross a chasm.
  3. Be creative and think outside the box. There are no limits to what you can draw in Draw Climber 2. Use your creativity to come up with new and innovative ways to solve the puzzles.

Tips for playing Draw Climber 2
