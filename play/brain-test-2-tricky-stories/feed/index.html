]]> Who Is? is a fun and challenging puzzle game that presents you with over a hundred tricky scenarios that will test your logic and thinking skills. In each level, you will be presented with a scenario and a series of clues. Your goal is to use the clues to figure out who or what is being described.

The scenarios in Who Is? can be quite varied, ranging from simple riddles to complex mysteries such as “Who is lying?”. Some scenarios may require you to think outside the box, while others may require you to pay close attention to the details.

One of the things that makes Who Is? so enjoyable is that there is no one right way to solve the puzzles. You can use any approach that you think will work. This makes the game accessible to players of all skill levels.

Another great thing about Who Is? is that it is constantly being updated with new levels. This ensures that there is always something new to challenge yourself with.

Overall, Who Is? is a great puzzle game that is sure to keep you entertained for hours on end. It is a must-play for fans of logic puzzles and brain teasers.

Here are some of the pros and cons of Who Is?:

Pros:

  • Over a hundred challenging puzzles
  • Varied scenarios that will test your logic and thinking skills
  • No one right way to solve the puzzles
  • Accessible to players of all skill levels
  • Constantly being updated with new levels

Cons:

  • Some puzzles can be very difficult
  • The game can be a bit repetitive at times

Overall rating: 4.5 out of 5 stars

Recommendation: Who Is? is a great puzzle game that is sure to keep you entertained for hours on end. It is a must-play for fans of logic puzzles and brain teasers.
