The game is simple to play but difficult to master. The player controls a ball that must be tapped through a series of obstacles. Each obstacle is a different color, and the ball must match the color of the obstacle in order to pass through it. If the ball hits an obstacle of a different color, the game ends.

The obstacles in Color Switch are constantly moving and changing, making the game challenging and addictive. The player must use quick reflexes and good timing to navigate through the obstacles.

Color Switch features multiple game modes, including endless, challenge, and race. Endless mode is the most popular game mode, and it challenges the player to see how long they can last. Challenge mode features a series of increasingly difficult levels. Race mode challenges the player to race against the clock to complete a series of levels.
