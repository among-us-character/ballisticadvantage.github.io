]]> Play Paper Minecraft Online

Paper Minecraft is actually a two-dimensional version of the legendary game Minecraft.

Before starting a game, you have to choose a variety of game characters.

Survival depends on your skill and ingenuity. So you have to collect resources and food from everywhere to survive. Of course, you need to build a town to live in.

In Paper Minecraft, like other Minecraft games, you have to face animals and different terrains such as forest, water, rock and fields. Mine all the stones and wood to make tools and use these tools to mine more advanced materials to build buildings.
