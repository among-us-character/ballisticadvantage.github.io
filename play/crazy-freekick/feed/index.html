]]> Play Penalty Kick Two-player Game

Football Storm Strike is an online sports game where you will be trying to score as many goals against your opponent as possible.

You can pick in between four modes. You can either play the tournament mode or try to score as many points in a time limit. In the next mode you will be able to play with a friend and compare your skills. The last mode is the training mode where you will simply be trying to score, kicking the ball as you go. While kicking, you need to set the trajectory of the ball and give it a spin so that you can outsmart the goalie.

Play Soccer Game on Desktop and Mobile

It is a game played in portrait and it’s playable on Desktop and Mobile

Score as many goals as you can in this online sports game.

Controls : Mouse – Interaction

Free Kicks on football match

In every football match, free kicks are moments of extreme pressure, in which a player has to kick the ball to another player or directly into the rival team’s goal. In this case, you have to shoot and trick the goalkeeper to score as many points as possible. You can change the direction of your shots to make them unstoppable. Play with your friends switching turns to see who rules the field. Have fun with Football Storm Strike!

Penalty Kick Online can either be played online or offline, it’s up to you! We do recommend that before you play online, you make sure that you are connected to a stable internet connection, and that you have played some offline games first before you go up against experienced players.
