Card Shuffle Sort Gameplay

The gameplay of Card Shuffle Sort is simple but addictive. You start each level with a board of cards arranged in random order. Your goal is to rearrange the cards so that all of the cards of the same color are stacked together. You can move cards by tapping and dragging them. However, there are a few rules that you must follow:

  • You can only move a card to an empty space or to the top of another card of the same color.
  • You cannot move a stack of cards.
  • You cannot move a card to a space that is already occupied by another card.

If you get stuck, you can use power-ups to help you progress. Power-ups can be used to remove cards from the board, shuffle the cards, or add new cards to the board.

Challenge

Card Shuffle Sort is a challenging game, especially as you progress through the higher levels. The levels are designed to test your problem-solving skills and strategic thinking. You will need to carefully plan your moves in order to solve the levels.
