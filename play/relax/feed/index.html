]]> Sheep Sheep Duck is a very novel casual multiplayer party interactive game.

You are free to choose and match the battle scenarios to your liking. The game has all kinds of cool scenes.

You can control the game character flexibly by tapping directly on the screen, and take more challenges here.

Show off your ability to defeat more enemies in this game, so you can get more weapons.

You can challenge yourself better with great strength, so you can unlock new game maps.

On PC = WASD and HJK to move and attack. On Mobile = Finger clicks the buttons to move and attack.
