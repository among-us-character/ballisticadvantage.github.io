]]> Slope Run is inviting you to participate in an exciting journey to restore memories of a ball.
The story starts when the ball which is actually an astronaut was on its way to explore new planets in the galaxy.
Unfortunately, it had an accident and lost its memories.
Therefore, it decides to traverse different planets to regain its memories. To reach these planets, the ball has to go through the space tunnel.
In this tunnel, it will encounter various dangerous obstacles such as unstable tiles and giant leaps. Help the ball overcome all dangers to get to the planets and find the instructions for the next steps. Good luck!

DESKTOP : Press the up arrow key to jump. – Press the left and right arrow keys to move left or right.

MOBILE -::Tap the up arrow on the screen to jump – Tap the left and right arrows on the screen to move left or right.
