The Goal: Merging Blocks to Glory

In 2020 Connect, your objective is to strategically place numbered blocks on a grid to create mergers. Here’s the key:

  • Merge Mania: Combine at least four blocks with the same number horizontally or vertically to make them merge into a single block with the sum of their values.
  • Bigger is Better: Merging more than four blocks of the same number in one move will earn you bonus coins!

Taking Control: Making Your Move

Use your trusty mouse to navigate this world of numbers. Here’s how to control the tiles:

  • Drag and Drop: Click on a block and hold your mouse button. Drag it to an empty space on the grid where you want to place it. Release the button to finalize the move.
  • Strategic Placement: Think ahead! Consider the potential mergers you can create with each move.

Tips to Become a Merging Master!

  • Plan Your Moves: Don’t just drop blocks randomly. Analyze the board and visualize the potential mergers you can create with each move.
  • Prioritize High Numbers: Focus on merging higher-numbered blocks first. This creates more space for new blocks and propels you towards the ultimate goal – the 8192 tile.
  • Utilize Corners: Corners can be strategic spots! Placing blocks here creates potential merges in two directions instead of just one.
  • Think Long Term: Don’t get caught up in making small merges just to clear space. Always consider how your move will impact future possibilities for high-value mergers.
  • Coins for Power: As you play, you’ll earn coins for merging more than four blocks. Save these coins for helpful power-ups that can help you in tricky situations (These power-ups might vary depending on the specific version of 2020 Connect you’re playing).

The board fills up with new blocks if you can’t make a merge. So, keep merging strategically and plan your moves carefully to dominate the game and reach the legendary 8192 tile!

With a little practice and these tips in mind, you’ll be a merging maestro in no time! Happy puzzling!

]]> Minesweeper 3D: A Classic Puzzle Game with a New Dimension

Minesweeper 3D is a fresh take on the classic Minesweeper puzzle game, bringing the familiar gameplay to a three-dimensional environment. the game features a variety of polyhedron shapes with an increasing number of sides, challenging players to apply their logic and deduction skills to uncover the hidden mines.

Gameplay Overview

The goal of Minesweeper 3D remains the same as its predecessor: to clear the board by uncovering all non-mine squares without triggering any explosions. Players click on squares to reveal their contents. If a number appears, it indicates the number of adjacent mines. If a mine is revealed, the game ends.

The 3D aspect adds a new layer of complexity to the game. Players can rotate the polyhedron to view different sides, making it more challenging to keep track of the mine locations. This twist forces players to think more strategically and carefully consider their moves.

Visuals and Audio

Minesweeper 3D features simple yet effective visuals. The polyhedron shapes are rendered in clean, bright colors, making it easy to distinguish between the different squares. The game also includes a variety of sounds that provide feedback to player actions, such as the clicking sound when a square is revealed and the explosion sound when a mine is triggered.

Controls

The controls in Minesweeper 3D are straightforward and easy to learn. Players use the left mouse button to uncover squares and the right mouse button to place flags, which mark suspected mine locations. The mouse wheel can be used to rotate the polyhedron.

Difficulty Levels

Minesweeper 3D offers four difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert. The Easy level features a small polyhedron with few mines, while the Expert level presents a larger, more complex shape with a greater number of mines. This range of difficulty levels caters to players of all skill levels.

Overall Impression

Minesweeper 3D is a fun and challenging puzzle game that successfully translates the classic Minesweeper experience to a 3D environment. The game’s simple visuals, intuitive controls, and varying difficulty levels make it accessible to a wide range of players. Whether you’re a seasoned Minesweeper enthusiast or a newcomer to the genre, Minesweeper 3D offers a fresh and engaging experience that will test your logic and deduction skills.

Tips for Success

  • Start with the Easy level to get familiar with the controls and gameplay mechanics.
  • Pay attention to the numbers on the squares. They provide valuable clues about the location of mines.
  • Use flags to mark suspected mine locations, but be careful not to overuse them.
  • Rotate the polyhedron frequently to get a better view of the different sides.
  • Don’t rush your moves. Take your time and think carefully before clicking on a square.
