]]> BitLife Game: Live and make the best decisions before you die!!!

You can marry the love of your life, have children and get a good education along the way. Or do you play choices that horrify your parents? You can enter a life of crime, fall in love or romance, start prison riots, smuggle suitcases and cheat on your spouse. You choose your story…

Discover how, little by little, life choices can add up to determine your success in the game of life. Interactive story games have been around for years. But this is the first text sim that truly synthesizes and simulates adult life.

BitLife Life Simulator is playable online as an HTML5 game, therefore no download is necessary.

Google Play | App Store

What’s the point of BitLife?

BitLife is a game where you have a randomized name and character, and are expected to live this new life to the fullest. The actions you make very much impact your life, and sometimes will choose whether you live or die.

Is BitLife ok for 12 year olds?

There is violence, sexual content, drug, and alcohol use in Bitlife that are not for young kids as this information may affect the mental and physical health of kids.

Is BitLife safe to play?

while older teens and adults who understand the tongue-in-cheek approach and mature content will likely get a kick out of this simulator, BitLife – Life Simulator is definitely not appropriate for kids and younger teens.
