]]> Narrow One is a 3D multiplayer archery game developed by Pelican Party Studios. It is a capture the flag game where two teams of players compete to capture the other team’s flag and bring it back to their own base. Players can only use a bow and arrows, so good aim and reflexes are essential.

The game is played in a medieval castle arena, with narrow passages and multiple levels. Players can use the environment to their advantage, taking cover behind walls or ambushing their opponents from above.

Narrow One is a fast-paced and exciting game that is easy to learn but difficult to master. It is available for free on web browsers and Android devices.

Tips for playing Narrow One:

  • Use the environment to your advantage. Take cover behind walls or ambush your opponents from above.
  • Communicate with your teammates. Coordinate your attacks and defend your flag together.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment. Try different weapons and tactics to see what works best for you.
  • Have fun! Narrow One is a great game to play with friends.
