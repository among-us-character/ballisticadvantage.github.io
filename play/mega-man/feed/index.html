]]> Vex 8 is a stickman parkour game developed by Amazing Adam. It is the eighth installment in the Vex series, and was released in October 2022. The game is available to play for free on Virlan games.

In Vex 8, players control a stickman who must navigate through a variety of challenging levels filled with obstacles. Players must use their parkour skills to jump, climb, and swing their way through the levels, while avoiding traps and enemies.

The game features a variety of different obstacles, such as spikes, saws, and moving platforms. Players must also collect stars to unlock new levels and power-ups.

Vex 8 is a challenging game that requires quick reflexes and good timing. It is also a very rewarding game, as players progress through the levels and unlock new abilities.

tips for playing Vex 8:

  • Use the arrow keys to move your stickman.
  • Press the spacebar to jump.
  • Press the shift key to sprint.
  • Press the control key to slide.
  • Press the Z key to climb.
  • Press the X key to swing.
  • Collect stars to unlock new levels and power-ups.
  • Be careful of traps and enemies.
  • Don’t be afraid to restart a level if you’re stuck.
