Run 3, known simply as “Run” for Android and more excitedly as “Run!!!” for iOS, is the third game of the Run trilogy.

It originally released to Google Play on December 11th, 2013. It would later come to Kongregate as a Flash game on June 5th, 2014.

On release it was little more than a remake of the original Run. Through a stream of consistent updates throughout the years, however, the title has evolved a distinct entry into the series with a plethora of unique gimmicks, characters, and an overarching plot.

When a wall or ceiling is touched, the character will “catch” onto the new surface and the gravity of the level will flip and the character will run on the new surface as though it were the floor. It is necessary to use this unique gameplay mechanic to complete many levels. However, some narrow, dark surfaces cannot be caught onto.
