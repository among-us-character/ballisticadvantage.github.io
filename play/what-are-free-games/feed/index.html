]]> What the Hen! is a free-to-play mobile idle RPG game developed by Charged Monkey. In the game, players collect and upgrade heroes to battle other players in the Arena and complete a challenging campaign mode. The game features a unique art style and over 70 different heroes to collect.

Here is a review guide for What the Hen!:

Gameplay

What the Hen! is a turn-based idle RPG game. Players build a team of heroes and then send them into battle against other players or enemies in the campaign mode. Battles are automatic, but players can use skills to help their heroes win.

The game features a variety of different game modes, including:

  • Campaign: Players progress through a series of levels, each with its own unique challenges and rewards.
  • Arena: Players battle against other players in 1v1 matches.
  • Clan Wars: Players team up with other members of their clan to battle against other clans.
  • Tower of Infinity: Players climb a tower of increasingly difficult enemies.
  • Events: The game regularly features special events with unique rewards.

Heroes

There are over 70 different heroes to collect in What the Hen!. Each hero has its own unique abilities and stats. Players can upgrade their heroes by using gold and other resources.

Heroes are divided into different factions, including:

  • Humans: Humans are well-rounded heroes with good stats in all areas.
  • Elves: Elves are agile and have high attack power.
  • Dwarves: Dwarves are tough and have high defense.
  • Goblins: Goblins are cunning and have unique abilities.
  • Robots: Robots are powerful and have special abilities.

Progression

Players progress in What the Hen! by completing levels in the campaign mode, winning Arena battles, and participating in events. As players progress, they will earn gold, other resources, and new heroes.

Players can use gold to upgrade their heroes, purchase items from the shop, and summon new heroes. Players can also use other resources to upgrade their heroes and unlock new abilities.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for What the Hen!:

  • Focus on upgrading your heroes: Upgrading your heroes is the best way to improve your team’s power.
  • Build a balanced team: Make sure your team has a good mix of different types of heroes.
  • Use your skills wisely: Your skills can be the difference between winning and losing a battle. Use them wisely to turn the tide of battle.
  • Participate in events: Events are a great way to earn rewards and new heroes.
  • Join a clan: Clans offer a variety of benefits, including access to clan wars and clan shops.
