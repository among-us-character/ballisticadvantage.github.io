  • 4 Wild Draw 4 cards
  • 4 Blank Cards

Each player draws a card. Player with the highest point value is the dealer. Shuffle the deck. Each player is dealt 7 cards.

Place the remaining cards facedown to form a draw pile. Turn over the top card of the draw pile to begin a discard pile. If the top card is a Wild or Wild Draw 4, return it to the deck and pick another card.

This card reverses direction of play. Play to. the left now passes to the right, and vice versa. If this card is turned up at the beginning of play, the player to the right now plays first, and play then goes to the right instead of left.
