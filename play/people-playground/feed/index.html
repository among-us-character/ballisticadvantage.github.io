]]> Funny Battle Simulator: A Game Review

Funny Battle Simulator is a game that lets you unleash your imagination and creativity in creating and commanding your own army of hilarious and absurd units. Whether you want to send a horde of cavemen, bears with miniguns, elephants, or even the mighty Zeus into battle, this game has it all.

Gameplay

The game is divided into two modes: campaign and sandbox. In campaign mode, you have to complete a series of levels with increasing difficulty and unlock new units along the way. Each level has a fixed budget and a maximum number of units you can place on the battlefield. You can choose from a variety of units, each with their own strengths, weaknesses, and abilities. Some units are more effective against certain enemies than others, so you have to use your strategy and tactics to win.

In sandbox mode, you can create your own custom scenarios and battles with no limits. You can set up any combination of units on both sides and watch them fight in a chaotic and hilarious spectacle. You can also adjust the gravity, time scale, and other settings to make the game more fun and challenging.

The game features realistic ragdoll physics and beautiful graphics that make the battles more immersive and entertaining. You can also control the camera movement and zoom in or out to get a better view of the action. You can even take control of some units, such as Zeus, and use their special powers to turn the tide of the battle.

Features

  • Large-scale and spectacular battles
  • Unique and funny units
  • Ragdoll physics of enemies
  • Beautiful graphics
  • Campaign and sandbox modes
  • Customizable settings
  • Free to play on browser

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • The game is very easy to play and enjoy. You just have to drag and drop the units on the battlefield and watch them fight.
  • The game is very addictive and replayable. You can try different strategies and combinations of units to see what works best.
  • The game is very humorous and amusing. You can witness some hilarious moments and situations, such as bears with miniguns shooting at cyclops or elephants stomping on archers.
  • The game is very creative and original. You can find some unique and bizarre units that you won’t see in any other game, such as flying sharks or UFOs.

Cons:

  • The game can be very laggy and buggy at times, especially when there are too many units on the screen or when the physics go wrong.
  • The game can be very repetitive and boring after a while, especially if you have unlocked all the units and completed all the levels.
  • The game can be very frustrating and unfair at times, especially when the enemy has more powerful or expensive units than you or when your units behave stupidly or randomly.
Conclusion

Funny Battle Simulator is a game that will make you laugh and have fun with its crazy and hilarious battles. If you are looking for a game that will challenge your creativity and strategy skills, this game is for you. If you are looking for a game that will relax you and entertain you with its absurdity and humor, this game is also for you. However, if you are looking for a game that will offer you a smooth and flawless experience, this game might not be for you.

Overall, Funny Battle Simulator is a game that deserves a try.
