]]> Mahjongg Dimensions Candy: 640 Second Challenge

Get ready to tantalize your taste buds and challenge your mind with Mahjongg Dimensions Candy 640 Seconds! This game combines the classic tile-matching fun of Mahjongg with a delicious candy theme and a thrilling time limit.

Here’s your guide to conquering this sugary challenge:

The Sweet Basics

  • Gameplay: Just like regular Mahjongg, your goal is to eliminate all the tiles from the board. You can only remove tiles that are identical and have at least one open side (not blocked by other tiles).
  • Candy Crush: This version features delectable candy-themed tiles instead of the usual Mahjongg symbols.
  • The Clock is Ticking: The twist? You only have 640 seconds (10 minutes and 40 seconds) to complete the board. That means quick thinking and strategic tile selection are crucial!

Tips for Time Travelers

  • Sharpen Your Vision: Quickly scan the board to identify matching candy tiles. Look for ones with open sides that you can readily remove.
  • Think in 3D: This version adds a new dimension! The board is 3D, so rotate it using your mouse or keyboard arrows to find hidden matching pairs behind or underneath other tiles.
  • Prioritize Special Tiles: Keep an eye out for special candy tiles that might offer unique abilities to help you clear the board faster.
  • Plan Your Moves: Don’t just remove random tiles. Try to anticipate future matches and remove tiles that will open up more options for later moves.
  • Work Quickly, But Stay Calm: With the time limit looming, it’s easy to get flustered. Take a deep breath, focus on the board, and make calculated moves to maximize your efficiency.

Bonus Tip: If you get stuck, some versions of the game might offer a hint button. Use it sparingly, as it can disrupt your focus and waste precious seconds.

With a dash of focus, a sprinkle of strategy, and a whole lot of candy-fueled fun, you’ll be a Mahjongg Dimensions Candy master in no time! So go forth, conquer the sugary board, and beat the clock!

]]> Ballistic is a free online game developed by Arkadium. It is a puzzle game where you have to launch balls at targets to destroy them. The game has over 100 levels, and each level gets progressively more difficult.

To play Ballistic, you use the mouse to aim and launch the balls. You can also use the keyboard to control the game. The left and right arrow keys move the cannon, and the space bar launches the ball.

The goal of the game is to destroy all of the targets on the screen. You can do this by launching balls at them directly, or by ricocheting the balls off of other objects.

How to play Ballistic

  1. Start by aiming the cannon at the targets. You can do this by moving the mouse or using the left and right arrow keys.
  2. Once you have aimed the cannon, click the mouse or press the space bar to launch the ball.
  3. The ball will bounce off of walls and other objects. Use this to your advantage to ricochet the ball into the targets.
  4. Destroy all of the targets on the screen to complete the level.

tips for playing:

  • Use the ricochet mechanic to your advantage. You can often destroy multiple targets with a single ball by ricocheting it off of walls and other objects.
  • Be careful not to launch the ball too hard. If you launch the ball too hard, it will bounce off of the walls and fly off the screen.
  • Use the power-ups to your advantage. Power-ups can help you to destroy targets more easily or to reach difficult areas.

Ballistic is a fun and challenging puzzle game that is suitable for all ages. It is a great way to pass the time or to improve your problem-solving skills.
