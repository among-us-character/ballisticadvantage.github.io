]]> Soar into a world of vibrant butterflies and colorful bubbles with Bubble Shooter Butterfly! This classic arcade game, reimagined for a fresh springtime experience, offers hours of addictive gameplay.

Bubble Shooter Butterfly is your next favorite bubble shooter game! It is a classic arcade game with a fresh look and new twists. With its bright butterflies and breezy gameplay this online butterfly game is perfect for Spring and Summer. The game includes 300 diverse levels, divided over beautifully serene day and night backgrounds. Release all the butterflies and achieve the highest scores!

Game Features:

  • 300 Levels: Conquer a variety of challenges across stunning day and night landscapes.
  • Beautiful Butterflies: Free these enchanting creatures from their bubbly captivity.
  • Breezy Gameplay: Enjoy a relaxing yet engaging experience.
  • Diverse Levels: Experience unique challenges and obstacles in every level.
  • High Scores: Compete with friends and aim for the top.

How to Play:

  1. Aim and Shoot: Use your mouse or finger to aim the bubble shooter and click or release to fire.
  2. Match Colors: Create groups of three or more bubbles of the same color to make them pop.
  3. Free the Butterflies: Clear the playing field to release the trapped butterflies.
  4. Switch Colors: Click the shooter to swap between the two available bubble colors.
  5. Earn Extra Points: The fewer bubbles remaining after clearing a level, the higher your score.
