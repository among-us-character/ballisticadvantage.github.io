Why PBS Kids is so popular?

PBS Kids is the brand for most of the children’s programming aired by the Public Broadcasting Service (PBS) in the United States.

Some public television children’s programs are not produced by PBS member stations or transmitted by PBS.

Instead, they are produced by independent public television distributors such as American Public Television, and are not labeled as “PBS Kids” programming, as it is mainly a programming block branding.

The target audience is children between the ages of 2 and 8. The network is also available in sub-Saharan Africa and Australia.
